Our Team

Sidhartha Todi

Sidhartha Todi is a BTech graduate from MIT Manipal. He was a management consultant at KPMG India. Being self driven and an ardent music lover, he quit his job and started 7Entertainment with a vision of providing quality artist and event management services. His experience speaks - worked with ARK Event management where he was responsible for organising concerts of international acts in Bangalore and has 6 years of cumulative experience in artist and event management.

Kunal Agarwal

Kunal Agarwal is an Electrical Engineer from IIT Bombay. A musician himself, Kunal plays the flute. He was previously employed with BG group. After quitting his job he joined as the founding member of 7Entertainment. A true believer in spreading joy through music, he is passionate about the vision that 7Entertainment represents. Kunal is the member of the team which manages all that is cool and geeky!

Vishal Rao

Vishal S Rao is a CA and graduated with an MBA from IIM Bangalore. He was previously a management consultant at KPMG India. He was a core organizing member of two college festivals at IIM Bangalore as the Logistics head and managed music events during these festivals. Trained in Carnatic classical music, and a passion for music as well as management led him to join 7Entertainment. Vishal brings his professionalism as well as a whacky sense of creativity to the team.
